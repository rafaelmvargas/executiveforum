Membership

The Executive Forum is comprised of professionals and entrepreneurs in a variety of businesses and welcomes alumni of other universities to membership. The Forum, which dates back to 1931, has provided its distinguished membership with camaraderie and an interesting array of business and non-business programs.

The group holds a series of four to five dinner events per year with high profile speakers and networking at the Torch Club in Manhattan. Membership, for the remaining 2016/17 which includes three events and a listing in the membership directory, is $395 and can be paid directly through PayPal, or by mailing a check, payable to "The Executive Forum" (Please see below for further details).


GUESTS
The discounted guest fee is $100 if the non-member has not attended a prior event this season. The regular guest fee is $125.

If a member purchases five (5) or more guest tickets before a dinner for admission to that particular event, the member will pay a discounted rate of $95 for each ticket.

PAYMENTS
